About Cobblemon

Cobblemon is a Minecraft mod that brings Pokémon into the game with a unique blend of survival and adventure elements. Players can catch, train, and battle Pokémon in an open-world setting while exploring Minecraft’s terrain.

Features:

  • Pokémon Spawning: Wild Pokémon naturally appear in different biomes.
  • Turn-Based Battles: Engage in strategic battles similar to the main Pokémon games.
  • Poképedia: An in-game book that provides Pokémon details and gameplay tips.
  • Starter Pokémon Selection: Choose a starter Pokémon to begin your journey.
  • Custom UI: Designed to match the aesthetic of both Pokémon and Minecraft.

For more details, visit the official Cobblemon page.
