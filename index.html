About Cobblemon

Cobblemon is a Minecraft mod that seamlessly integrates Pokémon into the game, offering a survival and adventure experience with turn-based battles and capturing mechanics.

Key Features

  • Pokémon Spawning: Discover and catch Pokémon across different biomes.
  • Turn-Based Battles: Battle against wild Pokémon and other trainers.
  • Poképedia: A built-in guide providing details on Pokémon and game mechanics.
  • Starter Selection: Choose a Pokémon to begin your journey.
  • Custom UI: A visually immersive experience inspired by Pokémon games.
